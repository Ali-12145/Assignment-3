Favorite Tourist Spots

Eiffel Tower, Paris

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It is named after the engineer Gustave Eiffel, whose company designed and built the tower.

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications that were built across the northern borders of ancient Chinese states and Imperial China as protection against various nomadic groups.

Colosseum, Rome

The Colosseum is an oval amphitheatre in the centre of the city of Rome, Italy. It is the largest ancient amphitheatre ever built, and is still the largest standing amphitheatre in the world today.

Statue of Liberty, New York

The Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture on Liberty Island in New York Harbor in New York City, in the United States.

Machu Picchu, Peru

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel located in the Eastern Cordillera of southern Peru on a mountain ridge 2,430 metres above sea level.

Sydney Opera House, Australia

The Sydney Opera House is a multi-venue performing arts centre at Sydney Harbour located in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia.
